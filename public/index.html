Location

33691 Bailey Meadow Rd
Palomar Mountain, CA 92060

DIRECTIONS
• Take Valley Center Rd to I-76 E
• Make a slight left onto Palomar Mountain Rd
• Continue up the mountain & make a left onto S Grade Rd
• When you see Mother's Kitchen, turn left onto State Park Rd
• Turn right onto Bailey Meadow Rd
• Follow Fern Trail all the way through to the end of the road

Attire

Dressy / Smart Casual

GUYS
• Button up, collared shirt
• Dress pants, dark jeans
• Dress shoes, boots
• Tie or other dapper accessories recommended

GALS
• Dress, skirt
• Nice pants with dressy top
• Flats, boots, wedges
• Heels highly discouraged (due to uneven terrain)

Dinner

Authentic carne asada and pollo asado street tacos with rice & beans + chips & salsa!

¡Delicioso!

A veggie option is available of soy chorizo, potatoes, onion & red bell pepper. Please specify on your RSVP if you would like veggie tacos so we can make arrangements with our caterer.

Travel Considerations

The trip up Palomar Mt is steep and twisty, so keep that in mind during your trek and allow enough time to get here before the festivities begin. You don't wanna miss it!

While parking is available at the venue and along the road, we encourage carpooling.

Accommodations

If you're traveling from out of town or just want to enjoy the weekend in the beautiful mountains, Baileys has several options for staying at or near the venue (including luxury campsites, if you're feeling especially adventurous).

Ask us for more info.

Other Info

• Children are welcome

• No handicap accessibility

• Again, avoid wearing heels
